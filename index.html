Medical Disclaimer
ClinicalTrials Finder · For Informational Use Only
Not Medical Advice
This app is provided for informational purposes only and does not constitute medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The information presented should never substitute for professional medical advice from a qualified healthcare provider.
Clinical Trial Information
Trial data is retrieved in real time from ClinicalTrials.gov, a registry maintained by the U.S. National Library of Medicine. Eligibility criteria shown may be summarized. Always review the full trial listing and consult the study team before making enrollment decisions.
Consult Your Physician
Participation in any clinical trial should only be considered after a thorough discussion with your treating physician or specialist, who can evaluate whether a specific trial is appropriate given your complete medical history, medications, and health status.
Privacy
This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal health information. Location data, if shared, is used solely to calculate distances to trial sites and is never recorded or sent to any server.
